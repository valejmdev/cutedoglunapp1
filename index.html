Welcome to my Website!

Let me introduce myself:

Hello there! I'm Luna, the Mini-Labrador + Ridgeback mix who's excited to welcome you to my world of wagging tails and playful adventures. Come along as I share my story and the paw-some services you can enjoy with me.

My Story:

I'm Luna, a spirited Mini-Labrador + Ridgeback mix, known for my love of cuddles, playtime, and exploring the great outdoors. My journey began when a family with a special needs child welcomed me into their home. Sadly, they had to move, leaving me in need of a new loving family. At the age of 3, I found my forever home with my current owner. It didn't take long for me to fall head over paws for Cesar, our other dog. Together, we had 10 adorable puppies, filling our home with joy and laughter. Following a challenging recovery from giving birth and the loss of dear Cesar to old age, I found solace and comfort in the loving care of my current owner. Cuddling is my absolute favorite pastime, closely followed by chasing my ball and indulging in some passionate wood chewing. I have a collection of cozy beds scattered around the house, but my favorite hangout spot is at my owner's grandma's place. There, I enjoy tasty chicken treats while keeping a watchful eye over our ducks and chickens, declaring them off-limits for my culinary adventures. One of my treasured activities is our woodland strolls to the nearby river, where I revel in splashing around and soaking up the natural surroundings. I also cherish the visits from two of my grown-up puppies, Flora and Ares, who bring extra fun and companionship to our pack. Socializing with other dogs is a highlight for me, as I thrive on making new friends and exchanging playful barks and tail wags. Join me for a tail-wagging good time filled with love, laughter, and unforgettable moments!

My loving Owner:

I would like to introduce my amazing owner to you. He's a 26-year-old human who spends a lot of time working on the phone and computer, but always makes sure to give me plenty of cuddles, even during his busy workdays. He takes breaks to play with me, ensuring I get the attention and fun I need. After work or on his days off, he takes me on long, relaxing strolls, allowing us to enjoy the fresh air and nature together. My owner is a food enthusiast, providing me with a variety of delicious meals and snacks to keep me happy and healthy. He even supplies me with the best wood pieces to chew on when he starts the oven, giving me a treat every time. To top it off, he spoils me by letting me snuggle up and sleep in bed with him, making me feel truly loved and cherished.

Bring your furry friend along for a playdate with me! Get ready for a fun-filled session of games, laughter, and bonding. Your dog will have the time of their life engaging in playful activities with me as their playmate. Watch as they build a special connection filled with joy and excitement.

Woodland Adventure Walk:

Join me, on an exhilarating woodland adventure walk. Prepare to explore the wonders of nature, breathe in the fresh air, and soak in the beauty of the great outdoors.
